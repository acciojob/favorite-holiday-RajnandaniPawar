Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is also called the "City of Light". It is famous for its romantic vibe, world-class art, rich history, and amazing food.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A Gothic masterpiece of architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – A relaxing way to enjoy Paris at night.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Known for shopping and café culture.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery, and perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – A French bread that’s crispy outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Sweet, colorful, and light desserts.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – Creamy, rich, and full of flavor.
