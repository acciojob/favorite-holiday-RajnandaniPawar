Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known as the "City of Light". It is famous for its romantic atmosphere, rich history, world-class art, and delicious cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous painting Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – Enjoy a scenic view of Paris at night.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous for shopping and café culture.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery, and perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – A traditional French bread, crispy outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Sweet, colorful, and delightfully light.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – Rich, creamy, and full of flavor.
